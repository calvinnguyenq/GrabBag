Info

After checking in, drop your bags off with our employees and they'll attach an RFID tag to your luggage. After that it will be loaded onto the plane and handled normally. When it comes time to pick up your bag from the carousel, a scanner will let you know when your bag has entered the carousel by sending a notification to your phone. No more crowding around the carousel constantly watching for your bag! After you have everything, just return the tag to our employees and you're good to go.
